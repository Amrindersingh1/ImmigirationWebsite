Application Review

If you have completed the application by yourself, we can do a professional review for completeness of the application requirements and assist you in the submission of your application. To do so we offer two plan for you to choose, depending at what stage your application is at.

Regular Plan:

We can assist in the review of your any application and supporting documents before your final submission to Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).

Features of the Regular Plan:

  1. Review of all the Forms
  2. Review of proof of employment (if applicable) and supporting documents
  3. Overall review and appropriate advice on quality of application

Premium Plan:
