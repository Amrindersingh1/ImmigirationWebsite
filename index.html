About Us



OCEANFRONT IMMIGRATION & CITIZENSHIP SERVICES Inc.

was founded by Ravneet Kaur with the aim of providing reliable, comprehensive, and competent immigration consulting services to the people who want pursue a new life in Canada. Ravneet moved to Canada in 2011 as a temporary resident and moved her way to being a proud Canadian Citizen.

She successfully completed her Immigration Practitioner Program (IPP) in honors and took entry to practice exam administered by ICCRC and became proud Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) in a good standing with the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council and hereby started her own practice to help all who seek to make Canada their new permanent home.
